## 🧠 About Me

<img align="right" width="300" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/212749447-bfb7e725-6987-49d9-ae85-2015e3e7cc41.gif" />

I'm **Rohith Vasanthan**, a final-year Computer Science Engineering student who genuinely loves solving real-world problems through software.

Most of what I build sits at the intersection of **AI and full-stack web development** — I enjoy designing scalable applications, then layering intelligence into them so they actually feel useful, not gimmicky.

A few things that drive how I work:

- 🔭 I like turning rough ideas into working products, not just prototypes.
- 🌱 I'm a continuous learner — always one course, one repo, or one late-night bug away from learning something new.
- 🤝 I'm slowly finding my footing in open source and enjoy contributing where I can.
- 🛠️ Modern web technologies + AI tooling is where I spend most of my time right now.
- 🎯 Long-term, I'm building toward both a startup idea and a strong industry career.

Nothing here is exaggerated — just a student who enjoys building, shipping, and iterating.
